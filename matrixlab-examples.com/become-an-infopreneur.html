Become an Infopreneur...


What's an infopreneur?

Infopreneur is a person whose primary business is gathering and distributing or selling just electronic information. This term is a new word derived from the words 'information' and 'entrepreneur'. This type of activity achieves  money giving away (or selling) information on the Internet. Infopreneurs use existing data and target an audience.

Before the Net became so handy, inforpreneurs sold their information in other mediums such as tapes, audio CDs, videos, and conferences... The classification of infopreneur has created a new style of business on the Internet, which allows anybody with a computer and an Internet connection to start a businesses by publishing information that may appeal to a specific market.


Everybody knows something about something...


There are generally two kinds of infopreneurs: those that sell information they have created on their own, and those that earn commissions from selling information that they know nothing about.
